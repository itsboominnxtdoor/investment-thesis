You are a senior equity research analyst. Given the following company information and SEC filing text, generate a structured business profile.

COMPANY: {company_name} ({ticker})
EXCHANGE: {exchange}
SECTOR: {sector}
INDUSTRY: {industry}

FILING TEXT:
{filing_text}

Respond with a JSON object containing exactly these fields:
- "description": A 2-3 paragraph overview of the company, its history, and market position.
- "business_model": How the company generates revenue and profits. Be specific about revenue streams.
- "competitive_position": Analysis of the company's position relative to peers, including market share insights.
- "key_products": A JSON array of strings listing the company's major products/services/segments.
- "geographic_mix": A JSON object mapping region names to approximate revenue percentage (e.g., {{"North America": 0.60, "Europe": 0.25, "Asia-Pacific": 0.15}}).
- "moat_assessment": One of: "wide", "narrow", "none".
- "moat_sources": A JSON array of strings describing the sources of competitive advantage (e.g., "brand", "network effects", "switching costs", "cost advantages", "intangible assets", "efficient scale").

Return ONLY the JSON object, no markdown fences or other text.
